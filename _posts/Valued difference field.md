A *valued difference field* is a [[Valued field]] $(K,v)$ together with an automorphism $\sigma: K \to K$ such that $\sigma[\mathcal O] \subseteq \mathcal O$. In particular, $\sigma$ induces an automorphism $\bar\sigma: k \to k$.
